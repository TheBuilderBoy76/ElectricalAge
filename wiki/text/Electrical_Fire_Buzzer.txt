}}The Electrical Fire Buzzer is a device that detects fire within it's range and emits a buzzing noise when detected. Takes batteries.
== How to use ==
To use, simply place a charged portable battery in the slot after right-clicking the Electrical Fire Buzzer. The Buzzer will activate after fire is detected.
