== Obtaining ==
<br />
Copper ingots can be obtained by smelting [[Copper Ore]]
<br />
== Usage ==
Copper Ingots is a crafting item which can be turned into cables, tools, and armor. Tools made with copper have less durability, but are more effective than those made from iron.
