The Battery Charger can be used to recharge electrical items. To power the device, you have to connect a 50v or 200v source to the charger with [[Electrical Cables|electrical cables]].

It can charge the following:

* [[Portable Battery]]
* [[Portable Battery|Portable Battery Pack]]
* [[Portable Condensator]]
* [[Portable Condensator|Portable Condensator Pack]]
* [[Small Flashlight]], 
* [[Portable Electrical Mining Drill]]
* [[Portable Electrical Axe]]
* [[X-Ray Scanner]]
* [[E-Coal Armour]].

To speed up charging process, you can use [[Machine Booster|machine boosters]]. The maximum number of [[Machine Booster|machine boosters]] per device is 5.
