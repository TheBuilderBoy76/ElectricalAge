A Portable Capacitor can storage electrical energy and can be used in small portable devices such as [[Digital Watch]] or to charge up [[X-Ray Scanner]].<br />
To provide more energy you can use Portable Capacitor Pack. In contrast to [[Portable Battery]], capacitors can store less energy but it can provide more energy in one time. <br />
It can be charged in [[Battery Charger]].

==Crafting and informations==

===Portable Capacitor===
* '''Charge power''' : 2000W
* '''Discharge power''' : 500W
* '''Maximal stored energy''' : 5000J
