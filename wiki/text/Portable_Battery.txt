The Portable Battery can storage electrical energy and can be used in small portable devices such as the [[Digital Watch]] or to charge up the [[X-Ray Scanner]]. Portable Battery Pack can provide more power to device.<br />
It can be charged in [[Battery Charger]].

==Crafting and informations==

===Portable Battery===
* '''Charge power''' : 500W
* '''Discharge power''' : 100W
* '''Maximal stored energy''' : 20000J
