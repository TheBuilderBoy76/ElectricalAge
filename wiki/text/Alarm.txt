==Usage==
Connect the '''Nuclear Alarm''' or '''Standard Alarm''' to a signal source with a value greater than 25 to activate it.

The '''Nuclear Alarm''' creates a mechanical air-raid siren type sound while the '''Standard Alarm''' generates an ascending tone.
