}}Data Loggers can measure the voltage of an electrical signal and plot the data in real time. Each one can store up to 256 points.

==Usage==

To measure the voltage level you have to connect [[Signal Cable]] to Data Logger.<br />
There is a couple of options in Data Logger menu. You can '''Reset''' a display, '''Pause''' or '''Run''' measurements or open '''Configuration''' options.<br />
In Configuartion menu you can set measurements '''Unit''', '''Sampling Period''', '''Maximal Y-axis''' value, '''Minimal Y-axis''' value or '''Print''' the data on paper.
