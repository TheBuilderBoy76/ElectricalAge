}}Electrical Light Sensor provides and electrical signal depending on strength of light which lights on the sensor.

If light shines on Light Sensor with strength equals 1, the sensor will give 3,33V (6,6%) of electricity.

The sensor works with light produced by [[Lamp|Lamps]] or Redstone as well as sunlight.
