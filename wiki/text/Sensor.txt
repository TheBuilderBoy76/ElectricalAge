Sensor is a Six-Node block outputting signal Voltage based on different things.
==Types of sensors==
* [[Light Sensor]] Detects Light
* [[Daylight Sensor]] Detects only natural light (made by sun). Torches or [[Lamp]]s won't work.
* [[Weather Sensor]] Detects Weather
* [[Anemometer Sensor]] Detects wind speed
* [[Entity Sensor]] Detects entity movement
* [[Fire Detector]] Detects Fire
